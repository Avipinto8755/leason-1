Football It is the most popular and popular team sport in the world. The game involves two teams, each of which aims to insert the ball into the other's goal. The whole team has eleven players.

Football is considered the most popular sport in the world: about 240 million people worldwide play football regularly

In professional games the teams play on a flat, rectangular grass surface. Goals are placed on both narrow sides of the field, and the points in the game are achieved by inserting the ball in its full extent into their area, an event known as the "goal valley" or "goal conquest". The team that scores more goals for the opponent's goal wins.
